SUMMARY

    Five high school students from different walks of life endure a Saturday detention under a power-hungry principal (Paul
    Gleason).The disparate group includes rebel John (Judd Nelson), princess Claire (Molly Ringwald), outcast Allison (Ally 
    Sheedy), brainy Brian (Anthony Michael Hall) and Andrew (Emilio Estevez), the jock. Each has a chance to tell his or her
    story, making the others see them a little differently -- and when the day ends, they question whether school will ever 
    be the same.
